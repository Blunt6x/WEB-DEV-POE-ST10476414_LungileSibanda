About Us

Ubuntu Youth Empowerment is a non-profit organisation based in Johannesburg, South Africa. Founded in 2018, it is dedicated to empowering disadvantaged youth through education, mentorship, and skills development programs. The organisation’s mission is to break the cycle of poverty by equipping young people with practical knowledge, confidence, and opportunities. Its vision is a society where every young person has access to the resources needed to thrive, regardless of their background. The primary target audience includes high school students, unemployed youth, and potential sponsors/volunteers who wish to contribute to community upliftment.
