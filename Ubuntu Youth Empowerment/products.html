Our Programs

Ubuntu Youth Empowerment offers a range of community-based initiatives:

  • After-School Tutoring – Providing academic support in Maths, Science, and English for high school learners.
  • Mentorship Program – Connecting students with professionals for career guidance and personal growth.
  • Skills Development Workshops – Training youth in computer literacy, coding, and digital design.
  • Community Outreach – Organising food drives, clothing donations, and wellness campaigns.
  • Leadership Camps – Building confidence, teamwork, and communication skills through weekend retreats.
