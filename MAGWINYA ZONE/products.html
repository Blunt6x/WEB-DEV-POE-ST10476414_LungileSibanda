Products

At Magwinya Zone, we serve a variety of delicious magwinya to suit every craving:

  • Plain Magwinya – Classic golden-brown fried dough, perfect on its own.
  • Cheese & Vienna Magwinya – Filled with melted cheese and vienna sausage.
  • Polony Magwinya – A township favorite with a savoury polony slice inside.
  • Mince Magwinya – Spicy minced beef filling for a hearty option.
  • Sweet Magwinya – Dusted with sugar, or filled with jam or chocolate for dessert lovers.
